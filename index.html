Our Products

Storm Drain Solutions

Efficient Storm Drain Waste Collection

The FillBask storm drain filter excels in trapping and collecting waste in urban drainage systems, ensuring efficient removal and prevention of debris from entering water bodies during rain seasons.

Easy Installation and Maintenance

Designed for simplicity, the FillBask storm drain filter offers easy installation and maintenance. Its user-friendly design allows quick setup and hassle-free maintenance, ensuring optimal performance in urban storm drainage systems.

Connectivity and Data Insights

FillBask goes beyond debris collection with its connectivity features, providing users with real-time data insights into storm drain conditions. Stay informed about waste levels and contribute to effective urban waste management.

Durable and Theft-Resistant Design

With a durable net and composite material construction, FillBask ensures a theft-resistant design for long-lasting storm drain waste management. Its robust features protect against unauthorized interference, preserving the integrity of the drainage system.

Simplifying Waste Management

The FillBask storm drain filter features an easy maintenance process, allowing for quick and straightforward emptying. Additionally, it is equipped with smart notification capabilities, sending alerts when the filter is full. This user-friendly design ensures efficient waste management and helps prevent issues such as clogging and flooding in urban storm drainage systems.

The Residential Smart Water Meter

Precision Measurement with Advanced Functionality

The Residential Smart Water Meter Smart Water Meter ensures precise and accurate water measurements, accompanied by advanced functionalities. This includes real-time water meter top-up and enhanced accuracy for billing purposes.

Intuitive Monitoring and Remote Configuration

Featuring a user-friendly digital display, the meter provides intuitive monitoring of water consumption. Users can conveniently set up tariffs remotely, gaining insights into usage patterns without complexity.

Effortless Remote Monitoring and Connectivity

The meter supports seamless remote monitoring, allowing users to access real-time water consumption data from anywhere. Its advanced connectivity features facilitate efficient data transmission and remote configuration options.

Tamper-Proof Design for Data Security.

The Genium Smart Water Meter employs a tamper-proof design, ensuring the integrity of consumption data. This design includes features for secure data handling and protection against unauthorized access.

Genium Smart Water Meter Communication with Genium Enable NB-IoT Gateway

Unlocking Advanced Connectivity

The Genium Smart Water Meter seamlessly communicates via the Genium Enable NB-IoT Gateway, utilizing Narrowband Internet of Things (NB-IoT) technology. This integration ensures efficient, low-power data transmission, enabling real-time water consumption insights and remote configuration for optimal efficiency. The Genium Enable NB-IoT Gateway propels the Genium Smart Water Meter into the realm of smart and sustainable water resource management.
