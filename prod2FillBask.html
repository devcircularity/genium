Efficient Storm Drain Waste Collection

The FillBask storm drain filter excels in trapping and collecting waste in urban drainage systems, ensuring efficient removal and prevention of debris from entering water bodies during rain seasons.

Easy Installation and Maintenance

Designed for simplicity, the FillBask storm drain filter offers easy installation and maintenance. Its user-friendly design allows quick setup and hassle-free maintenance, ensuring optimal performance in urban storm drainage systems.

Connectivity and Data Insights

FillBask goes beyond debris collection with its connectivity features, providing users with real-time data insights into storm drain conditions. Stay informed about waste levels and contribute to effective urban waste management.

Durable and Theft-Resistant Design

With a durable net and composite material construction, FillBask ensures a theft-resistant design for long-lasting storm drain waste management. Its robust features protect against unauthorized interference, preserving the integrity of the drainage system.
